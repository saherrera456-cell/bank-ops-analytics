# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
This project is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience.

## Expected Behavior
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Accept responsibility and apologize when necessary.
- Focus on what is best for the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or insults.
- Derogatory comments based on personal characteristics.
- Public or private harassment.
- Trolling, demeaning, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Reporting Issues
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the repository owner privately at the email listed in the repository profile.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take action including warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from contributing to this repository if behavior is unacceptable.

---

Thank you for helping keep this community open and respectful.
